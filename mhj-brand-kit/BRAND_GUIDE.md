---

## Logo Usage Rules

- **Minimum clear space**: 50% of logo height on all sides
- **Minimum size**: Wordmark 80px wide, icon 24px
- **Approved backgrounds**: Cream (#FAF8F5), white (#FFFFFF), or sufficiently dark solid colors
- **Never**: drop shadows, gradients, rotation, stretching, strokes over 1px, complex/busy backgrounds, inserting extra text inside logo
